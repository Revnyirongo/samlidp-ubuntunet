# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

Contributors are expected to keep discussions respectful, technical, and focused on improving the software.

## Unacceptable Behaviour

- harassment or discrimination
- abusive or insulting language
- publishing private information without consent
- bad-faith disruption of community or review processes

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions and interactions that do not meet this standard.

## Reporting

Report conduct concerns to:

- revelation.nyirongo@ubuntunet.net

All reports will be handled as confidentially as practical.
